Our Story

Giovanni Mozzarelli - Papa!

In the south of Italy cooking starts at an early age. Kids are always around mothers, aunts and grandmothers while preparing food. Giovanni has fond childhood memories of making pizzas with his mum and brother on the kitchen table, pressing out the dough with their small fingers and decorating with delicious ingredients. The way they made the pizzas then is the same technique Giovanni uses at his pizzeria today.

His food has garnered praise from many sources over the years and he has continued to bring Italian culture out through his food.
